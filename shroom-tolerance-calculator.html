Mushroom FAQ

What is Psilocybin?

Psilocybin is a naturally occurring psychedelic compound produced by more than 200 species of fungi (magic mushrooms). It converts to psilocin in the body, interacting with serotonin receptors.

How long does it last?

A typical trip lasts 4-6 hours. The peak usually occurs 1-2 hours after ingestion. Residual effects (afterglow) can last for another 4-8 hours.

What is Tolerance?

Tolerance builds up almost immediately after ingestion. Taking the same dose the next day will have significantly reduced effects. It takes about 5-7 days for tolerance to fully reset to baseline.

Set & Setting?

Crucial for a good experience. Set refers to your mindset. Setting refers to your environment. Optimize both for safety and enjoyment.
