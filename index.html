Psychedelic FAQ

What is LSD?

Lysergic acid diethylamide (LSD) is a potent psychedelic substance. It interacts with serotonin receptors in the brain, altering perception, mood, and cognitive processes.

How long does it last?

A typical trip lasts 8-12 hours. The peak usually occurs 2-4 hours after ingestion. Residual effects (afterglow) can last for another 6-12 hours.

What is Tolerance?

Tolerance builds up almost immediately after ingestion. Taking the same dose the next day will have significantly reduced effects. It takes about 12-14 days for tolerance to fully reset to baseline.

Is it Safe?

Physically, LSD is considered non-toxic and non-addictive. However, it can be psychologically intense. "Bad trips" can occur. Always test your substance to avoid dangerous imposters like NBOMes.

Set & Setting?

Crucial for a good experience. Set refers to your mindset. Setting refers to your environment. Optimize both for safety and enjoyment.
